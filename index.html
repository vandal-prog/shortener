FAQ

What is a URL shortener?

A URL shortener is a tool that takes a long URL (Uniform Resource Locator) and creates a shorter, more condensed version. It helps to make links more manageable, easier to share, and visually appealing.

Are shortened URLs permanent?

Most URL shorteners aim to provide permanent redirection for the shortened links. However, it's essential to check the terms and conditions of the specific URL shortener service you are using. Some shorteners may have limitations on link expiration or offer customization options that could affect the link's longevity.

Can I track the performance of my shortened links?

Yes, many URL shorteners provide analytics and tracking features. These allow you to monitor the number of clicks, geographical information about the clicks, referral sources, and other relevant data. Tracking statistics can help you measure the effectiveness of your marketing campaigns or understand user behavior related to the links you share.
